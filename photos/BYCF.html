Bangladesh Youth Cadets Forum (BYCF) is a youth organization in Bangladesh that aims to promote leadership, discipline, physical fitness, and patriotism among young people in the country. The organization provides training and development opportunities for its members, who are mostly students and young professionals, through various programs and activities such as camping, adventure sports, community service, and cultural events. The BYCF also collaborates with other national and international organizations to create youth development opportunities and promote global peace and harmony.
